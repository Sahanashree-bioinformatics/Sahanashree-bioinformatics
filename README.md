## Featured Projects

### Computational Approach to Gut Microbiome Associations in Kidney Stone Disease

Investigated gut microbiome alterations in kidney stone disease using shotgun metagenomic datasets from healthy and stone-forming individuals.

The project focused on identifying disease-associated microbial dysbiosis, altered abundance patterns, functional changes, and potential microbial contributors linked to kidney stone disease.

**Tools:** SRA Toolkit, Bowtie2, MetaPhlAn, HUMAnN, MaAsLin2, DKI, Python, R, Linux

---

### Comprehensive Variant Profiling of SARS-CoV-2 in New York State

Developed a bioinformatics pipeline for SARS-CoV-2 whole genome variant profiling using publicly available NGS datasets.

The project involved mutation pattern analysis to support regional genomic surveillance and viral evolution interpretation.

**Tools:** SRA Toolkit, SAMtools, FreeBayes, SnpEff, Python, Linux

---

### Molecular Docking Analysis of SARS-CoV-2

Performed structure-based molecular docking to screen compounds against SARS-CoV-2 protein targets.

The project included protein preparation, ligand preparation, docking analysis, binding affinity interpretation, and visualisation of molecular interactions.

**Tools:** PyRx, AutoDock, PyMOL, Discovery Studio

---

### Comparative and Functional Insight into Bile Acid-Transforming Enzymes in IBD and Colorectal Cancer

Analysed large-scale shotgun metagenomic datasets from IBD and colorectal cancer cohorts to compare gut microbial composition across UC, CD, and CRC groups.

The project used taxonomic profiling, functional profiling, and network-based keystone species analysis to identify disease-specific microbiome patterns.

**Tools:** MetaPhlAn, HUMAnN, MaAsLin2, DKI, Python, R, Linux

---

### Antimicrobial Activity of *Murraya koenigii*

Completed a wet-lab internship project evaluating the antimicrobial activity of *Murraya koenigii* leaf extracts.

The work involved extract preparation, nutrient agar preparation, plate pouring, bacterial inoculation, extract loading, aseptic handling, and zone of inhibition measurement.

**Skills:** Microbiology, antimicrobial screening, agar diffusion assay, aseptic technique, result documentation
